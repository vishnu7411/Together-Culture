Membership Details

1. Community Members

Description: These are general members who are part of the Together Culture community. They participate in events, workshops, and activities but do not have access to specialized resources or workspaces.

Benefits:

  • Access to digital content modules.
  • Ability to book events and workshops.
  • Participation in the timebank and skills library.
  • Networking opportunities with other members.

2. Key Access Members

Description: These members have enhanced access to Together Cultureâ€™s resources and facilities. They are likely more involved in the community and may contribute as volunteers or leaders.

Benefits:

  • All benefits of Community Members.
  • Priority booking for events and workshops.
  • Access to exclusive resources or tools.
  • Ability to post on the Connections Board (e.g., offering skills or requesting help).

3. Creative Workspace Members

Description: These members have access to physical or digital creative workspaces provided by Together Culture. They are likely artists, entrepreneurs, or professionals who need dedicated space to work on projects.

Benefits:

  • All benefits of Key Access Members.
  • Access to creative workspaces (e.g., studios, co-working areas).
  • Use of specialized equipment or tools.
  • Opportunities to showcase work or collaborate on community projects.
